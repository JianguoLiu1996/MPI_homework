# KSP_NORM_NATURAL
Compute the 'natural norm' of residual sqrt((b - A*x)*B*(b - A*x)) and pass that to the convergence test routine. This is only supported by  `KSPCG`, `KSPCR`, `KSPCGNE`, `KSPCGS`, `KSPFCG`, `KSPPIPEFCG`, `KSPPIPEGCR`
