# TSSSPRKS3
Optimal third order SSP Runge-Kutta, low-storage, c_eff=(PetscSqrtReal(s)-1)/PetscSqrtReal(s), where PetscSqrtReal(s) is an integer Pseudocode 2 of Ketcheson 2008
