# SNES_NORM_INITIAL_FINAL_ONLY
Compute the function and its L2 norm on only the initial and final iterations. 


## Note
This method combines the benefits of `SNES_NORM_INITIAL_ONLY` and `SNES_NORM_FINAL_ONLY`.
