# SNESPATCH
Solve a nonlinear problem or apply a nonlinear smoother by composing together many nonlinear solvers on (often overlapping) patches 


## References

- **** -*** Peter R. Brune, Matthew G. Knepley, Barry F. Smith, and Xuemin Tu, "Composing Scalable Nonlinear Algebraic Solvers", SIAM Review, 57(4), 2015
