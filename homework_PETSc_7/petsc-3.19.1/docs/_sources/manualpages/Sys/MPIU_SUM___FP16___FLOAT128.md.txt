# MPIU_SUM___FP16___FLOAT128
MPI_Op that acts as a replacement for `MPI_SUM` with custom `MPI_Datatype` `MPIU___FLOAT128`, `MPIU___COMPLEX128`, and `MPIU___FP16`. 


## Developer Note
This should be unified with `MPIU_SUM`


## See Also
 `MPIU_REAL`, `MPIU_SCALAR`, `MPIU_COMPLEX`
